NHL GAME SIMULATOR

by Victor Ruggi

This simulator does not take into account current NHL standings and injuries, nor does it predict that a goalie will get tossed out of a game while it is in progress. I did my best when it came to pairing players to their lines. In the end, the final score is what really matters. Skaters are the only entities that can score goals or record assists, since "goalie goals" are very rare, as are goalies getting assists, although the latter is slightly more common.
