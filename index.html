NHL Game Simulator

by Victor Ruggi

This simulator does not take into account current NHL standings or lines. In the end, the final score is what really matters. Skaters are the only entities that can score goals or record assists, since "goalie goals" are very rare, as are goalies getting assists, although the latter is slightly more common.
"Other (insert team here)" when it comes to goals and assists means any player who joins the team roster later on in the season from either the juniors or the Draft.
Click here to view all the team rosters. If you are unfamiliar with the NHL, you will need to know the names of each player in the NHL and how they are spelled, as it will come in handy later.
Once the simulation is done, you can view a box score of the result at the very bottom of the page.
